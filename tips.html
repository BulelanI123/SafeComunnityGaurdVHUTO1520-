Safety Tips

Home Security Tips

Secure Your Doors and Windows

Always lock all doors and windows, even when you're at home. Consider installing deadbolts and window locks.

Install Motion Sensor Lights

Motion-activated lights can deter potential intruders and alert you to movement around your property.

Use a Home Security System

Consider installing alarms, cameras, or smart home devices to monitor your property.

Neighborhood Watch Tips

Get to Know Your Neighbors

Build relationships with neighbors to create a supportive community network.

Report Suspicious Activity

If you see something unusual, report it immediately to authorities and your neighbors.

Organize Watch Groups

Form neighborhood watch groups to patrol and monitor your area regularly.

Personal Safety Tips

Stay Aware of Surroundings

Be mindful of your environment and avoid distractions like using your phone while walking.

Avoid Walking Alone at Night

Walk with a friend or use well-lit, populated routes when possible.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, leave the situation and seek help immediately.

Online Scam Awareness

Verify Before Clicking

Don't click on suspicious links or download attachments from unknown sources.

Use Strong Passwords

Create unique, complex passwords and use two-factor authentication.

Be Cautious with Personal Information

Never share sensitive information like your address or financial details online.
